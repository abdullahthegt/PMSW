💼 USE CASE EXAMPLE: ADAPTIVE CRUISE CONTROL (ACC) PROJECT

SCENARIO: Planning Sprint 12 with 45 story points

VELOCITY ANALYSIS:
• Historical average: 42 SP
• Volatility: ±10 SP
• Safe velocity (80% confidence): 38 SP
• Insight: 45 SP is risky (40% success probability)
• Recommendation: Plan for 35-40 SP

RESOURCE ALLOCATION:
• 35 tasks across 12 team members
• Safety Manager is critical bottleneck
• No developers for "Hardware Integration" skill
• 3 developers at 85%+ capacity
• Insight: Skill gap + resource constraint

BOTTLENECKS IDENTIFIED:
✗ No coverage for "Hardware Integration" skill
⚠ Safety Manager: only 50% available (shared across projects)
⚠ Developer Team: 3 of 7 at high utilization

DECISION SUPPORT:
1. Scope Reduction: Remove 5 low-priority SP → 40 SP
2. Resource Negotiation: +10 hours Safety Manager capacity
3. Cross-training: Upskill 1 developer in Hardware Integration
4. Result: 80% success probability
